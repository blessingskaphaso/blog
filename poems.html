The Morning Blessing

" Dawn breaks with golden light divine,
A blessing from the throne on high,
Each ray a thread in God's design,
That paints hope across the sky. "
" Dawn breaks with golden light divine,
A blessing from the throne on high,
Each ray a thread in God's design,
That paints hope across the sky.

The morning dew upon the grass,
Reflects the glory of His face,
As shadows of the night now pass,
We wake to witness boundless grace.

O Lord, who hung the stars in space,
And set the sun to mark our days,
We lift our hearts in grateful praise,
For every dawn reveals Your ways.

In stillness now, before the rush,
Of duties calling us to move,
We hear Your whisper in the hush,
A gentle word of endless love. "

A heartfelt poem of gratitude that captures the sacred beauty of each new day, reminding us of God's constant presence in the simple miracle of sunrise.

Ode to the Eucharist

" In bread transformed, in wine made new,
The mystery of love unfolds... "
" In bread transformed, in wine made new,
The mystery of love unfolds,
Emmanuel, forever true,
A gift more precious than all gold.

Upon the altar, white and pure,
The sacrifice renewed each day,
Your presence here, so real, so sure,
In simple forms You choose to stay.

Not mere memorial of the past,
But living presence, here and now,
Through ages meant forever last,
Before Your throne we humbly bow.

O Sacred Body, broken, shared,
O Precious Blood, outpoured for all,
By this great meal our souls are spared,
You lift us each time that we fall. "

Profound reflections on the sacred mystery of the Eucharist, exploring the transformation that occurs both in the elements and in our hearts.

Journey of Grace

" Each step upon this sacred ground,
Where pilgrims' prayers have worn the stone... "
" Each step upon this sacred ground,
Where pilgrims' prayers have worn the stone,
In silence deep, I hear the sound,
Of grace that calls me to my own.

The path is long, the journey steep,
With valleys dark and mountains high,
Yet promises Your love will keep,
Guide every tear and every sigh.

When weary feet can walk no more,
Your strength becomes my staff and stay,
You've walked this road of pain before,
And know the cost of every way.

So onward still, by faith not sight,
Through shadows deep and storms that rage,
For at the end, eternal light,
Awaits beyond this earthly stage. "

A spiritual pilgrimage captured in verse, tracing the path of faith through life's challenges and celebrating the grace that guides us forward.

The Beauty of Silence

" In silence deep, the soul finds rest,
Where God's own whisper can be heard... "
" In silence deep, the soul finds rest,
Where God's own whisper can be heard,
No human voice, no earthly test,
Just tender love without a word.

The world outside with all its noise,
Fades distant like a troubled dream,
And in this peace, the heart finds poise,
In waters of a quiet stream.

Here prayer is not with words arrayed,
But simple presence, soul to soul,
In contemplation we are made,
To see beyond and grasp the whole.

O teach us, Lord, to still our hearts,
To cease from striving, let You be,
The silence where new life imparts,
The gift of deeper mystery. "

An exploration of the sacred nature of silence, where the divine voice speaks most clearly to the attentive heart in moments of quiet contemplation.

Silence Contemplation Divine Voice

Reflections on the Psalms

" Ancient words that still ring true,
David's heart in verses penned... "
" Ancient words that still ring true,
David's heart in verses penned,
Speak comfort fresh as morning dew,
A shepherd-king, our trusted friend.

He knew the heights of joyful praise,
And depths where anguish tears the soul,
Through all his dark and troubled days,
His songs made wounded spirits whole.

"The Lord's my shepherd," still we sing,
Through valleys shadowed, steep and long,
These timeless words of hope we bring,
The same eternal, ancient song.

O may we learn like David learned,
To pour our hearts before the throne,
And find, as he so deeply earned,
We're never, ever left alone. "

Finding comfort and wisdom in the timeless prayers of David, connecting our contemporary struggles with the eternal human search for divine comfort.

Faith and the Unknown

" Beyond the veil of what we see,
Faith ventures forth on wings of trust... "
" Beyond the veil of what we see,
Faith ventures forth on wings of trust,
Into the vast uncertainty,
Where doubt and hope both turn to dust.

We cannot prove with mind alone,
The mysteries that faith reveals,
Nor measure love on scales of stone,
Nor quantify what spirit feels.

Yet in the darkness, still we find,
A light that logic can't explain,
A peace surpassing heart and mind,
That turns our questioning to gain.

So let us walk the path unknown,
With trembling hearts but steady feet,
For faith grows strongest when we've grown,
Through mystery to make complete. "

Embracing mystery as an integral part of our spiritual journey, exploring how uncertainty can deepen rather than diminish our relationship with the divine.

Faith Mystery Trust
