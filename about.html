About Me

Servant of God, Shepherd of Souls

Rev. Fr. Geraldo Bleza Malota

Dedza Diocese, Malawi

I am Rev. Fr. Geraldo Bleza Malota, a diocesan Catholic priest from Dedza Diocese in Malawi. With a deep-rooted passion for theology, poetry, and Christian reflection, I dedicate this space to sharing my insights and literary works with the world.

Through these writings, I hope to inspire, uplift, and engage readers in meaningful contemplation, spiritual growth, and personal transformation rooted in faith and humanity. My journey in priesthood has been one of continuous learning, service, and dedication to the spiritual well-being of all who seek guidance and inspiration.
