About Me

Servant of God, Shepherd of Souls

Rev. Fr. Geraldo Bleza Malota

Dedza Diocese, Malawi

I am Rev. Fr. Geraldo Bleza Malota, a diocesan Catholic priest from Dedza Diocese in Malawi. With a deep-rooted passion for theology, poetry, and Christian reflection, I dedicate this space to sharing my insights and literary works with the world.

Through these writings, I hope to inspire, uplift, and engage readers in meaningful contemplation, spiritual growth, and personal transformation rooted in faith and humanity. My journey in priesthood has been one of continuous learning, service, and dedication to the spiritual well-being of all who seek guidance and inspiration.

Faith is not merely a destination but a journey that one requires courage, perseverance, and an open heart. Through my articles and poems, I seek to accompany you on this journey, offering reflections drawn from Scripture, tradition, and the lived experiences of our shared humanity.

Areas of Focus

Theology

Deep exploration of Catholic doctrine and spiritual teachings to illuminate the path of faith

Poetry

Creative expression through verse and spiritual reflection that touches the soul

Ministry

Pastoral care and spiritual guidance for the faithful seeking God's presence

My Journey

Early Calling

From a young age, I felt drawn to serve God and His people, inspired by the witness of faithful priests and the beauty of the Church's liturgy.

Seminary Formation

Years of study and spiritual formation prepared me for the sacred ministry of priesthood, deepening my understanding of theology and pastoral care.

Priestly Ordination

Ordained a priest for Dedza Diocese, I committed my life to serving God's people through the sacraments, preaching, and spiritual accompaniment.

Ongoing Ministry

Today, I continue to serve through pastoral work, writing, and sharing reflections that inspire faith and hope in the hearts of believers.
