About Me

I am Rev. Fr. Geraldo Bleza Malota, a diocesan Catholic priest from Dedza Diocese in Malawi. With a deep-rooted passion for theology, poetry, and Christian reflection, I dedicate this space to sharing my insights and literary works with the world.

Through these writings, I hope to inspire, uplift, and engage readers in meaningful contemplation, spiritual growth, and personal transformation rooted in faith and humanity.
